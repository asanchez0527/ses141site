How can we reduce our impact?

  • Buy electric vehicles
    • These tend to be more energy efficient that gas cars and have less carbon footprint
  • Carpool when possible
    • A large percentage of our energy usage is transportation so the less cars on the road the better
  • Upgrade A/C Units
    • Modern a/c units are held to higher energy efficiency standards and do a better job cooling the same space
  • Get a new water heater
    • Tankless water heaters are around 24-34% more efficient
